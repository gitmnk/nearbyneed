# NearbyNeed

NearbyNeed is a community-driven platform designed to connect people who need help with those who can provide it, right in their local neighborhood. Whether it's borrowing a tool, getting help with a small task, or sharing resources, NearbyNeed makes it easy to ask for and offer support within your community.
